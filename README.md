# Unpack & Play 3D - Family Edition v1.0.0

A cozy 3D "decorate a room" sandbox game designed for family fun. Unpack boxes, place items, and create your own cozy space.

## How to Play

-   **Drag & Drop:** Click and drag items from the catalog to place them in the room.
-   **Rotate:** Use the **Q** and **E** keys to rotate selected items. Hold **Shift** for fine-tuned rotation.
-   **Duplicate:** Press **D** to duplicate the selected item.
-   **Delete:** Press **Delete** or **Backspace** to remove the selected item.
-   **Undo/Redo:** Use **Ctrl+Z** to undo and **Ctrl+Y** to redo your actions.
-   **Grid Snap:** Toggle grid snapping with the **G** key.
-   **Photo Mode:** Press **P** to enter Photo Mode and hide the UI for clean screenshots.

## Features

-   **Smooth Controls:** Enjoy a frustration-free experience with intuitive drag-and-drop controls and surface-aware snapping.
-   **Save & Load:** Save your creations in one of three named slots ("Mom", "Dad", "Kids") or use the "Quick Save" feature.
-   **Beautiful Visuals:** The game features realistic HDRI lighting and cinematic ACESFilmic tone mapping for a cozy and inviting atmosphere.
-   **Kids Mode:** A simplified mode with larger UI elements, forced snapping, and a limited catalog, perfect for younger players.
-   **Randomize Cozy:** Get inspired with the "Randomize Cozy" button, which places a few items in the scene to get you started.
